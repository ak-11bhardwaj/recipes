Veg Sandwich Recipe

    Ingredients

  1. Bread
  2. Cabbage
  3. Onion
  4. Carrots
  5. Capcicum
  6. Cheese

Procedure

Toast the Bread

Smear butter or ghee on the bread and toast them until a golden color appears

Fry the Vegies

Add oil on the pan and fry the vegies

Fill the Vegetables in bread

Place the toasted slices and transfer the veggies over them. If you wish to have cheese, then sprinkle now.

Serve the Sandwich
