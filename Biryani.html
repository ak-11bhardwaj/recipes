Veg Biryani Recipe

    Ingredients

  1. Onion(Chopped)
  2. Tomato(Slices)
  3. Garlic
  4. Chopped Vegetables
  5. Rice
  6. Water
  7. Salt
  8. Dry Masalas
  9. Others Spices

Procedure

ROAST THE DRY MASALAS

Prepare all the ingredients in advance. Heat ghee in a pan and roast dry masalas like cinnamon, cloves, green cardamom, star anise, peppercorns, and black cardamom. Add onions and saut√© till they turn golden-brown.

FRY THE VEGEIES

Add the tomatoes and cook till they are soft. Add all the other vegetables and mix well.

ADD RICE AND OTHER SPICES

Add water, crumbled MAGGI cubes, mint leaves and coriander leaves to the vegetable mixture. Mix well. Add salt to taste and bring to boil. Then, add rice.

SERVE THE BIRYANI
